About us

Welcome to our GIS-based mapping application, developed under Jio’s initiative, offers advanced and user-friendly geospatial tools. Key features include secure login, scale measurement, live location tracking, zoom controls, and a robust place search system. Users can explore areas with 360° street view and access detailed information by clicking on the map. Designed for seamless interaction, the application provides intuitive functionality for navigation and planning. This project reflects Jio’s commitment to delivering innovative mapping solutions.
